Full Description

繁體中文

《肥仔將軍大冒險》係一款快節奏 2D side-scrolling action survival game。你要操控肥仔將軍喺高壓戰場中閃避飛彈、擊退敵人、蓄力斬擊，並喺每次 Boss fight 入面搵到反擊時機！

遊戲重點：

  • 快節奏生存戰：閃避、走位、反擊，一秒都唔可以鬆懈
  • 蓄力攻擊系統：按住儲力，放手打出強力斬擊
  • Boss 對決：每場 Boss 戰都有節奏變化同特殊攻擊
  • 關卡遞進：越後期越刺激，敵人密度與壓力持續上升
  • 天氣與場景變化：雨、霧、風等效果帶來唔同戰鬥體感
  • 容易上手，持續進步：簡單操作，但要高專注先可以生存更耐

玩法特色：

  • 側向動作（side-scrolling）節奏明快
  • 強調 action combat 手感與 survival 決策
  • 適合鍾意高壓挑戰、追求操作成長嘅玩家

你可以生存到幾耐？即刻加入《肥仔將軍大冒險》，挑戰極限，擊破 Boss！

English

Fatty General's Great Adventure is a fast-paced side-scrolling action combat survival game. Dodge incoming bullets, strike back with precise timing, charge up powerful slashes, and survive intense boss fights.

Key Features:

  • Fast survival gameplay: dodge, reposition, counterattack, and stay focused
  • Charge attack system: hold to charge, release for powerful burst damage
  • Boss fights with patterns: each encounter has unique pacing and special attacks
  • Progressive stages: higher stages bring faster pressure and denser enemy waves
  • Dynamic weather and atmosphere: rain, fog, wind, and visual effects that change combat feel
  • Easy to learn, hard to master: simple controls with skill-based depth

Why you'll like it:

  • Responsive side-scrolling combat
  • Action combat plus survival decision-making
  • Great for players who enjoy challenging, high-pressure gameplay

How long can you survive? Download Fatty General's Great Adventure and take on the boss today.
