玩法介紹（中文）

目標：喺密集飛彈與敵人攻勢中生存，打敗每關 Boss，推進更高關卡。

基本操作

  • 左下按鈕：跳躍
  • 右下按鈕：普通攻擊
  • 長按攻擊：儲氣，釋放強力招式

核心節奏

  • 先走位閃避，再用普通攻擊清障
  • 留儲氣技打關鍵時機（例如 Boss 壓近）
  • 每關難度逐步提升，重點係穩定節奏

進階技巧

  • 避免長時間停在同一高度
  • 先保命再輸出，唔好貪刀
  • Game Over 後可透過 Rewarded 廣告重開（如適用）

How to Play (English)

Goal: survive waves of bullets/enemies, defeat each stage boss, and push to higher stages.

Controls

  • Left button: Jump
  • Right button: Normal Attack
  • Hold Attack: Charge and release a stronger move

Core Loop

  • Dodge first, attack second
  • Use charge attack at high-pressure moments
  • Difficulty scales gradually per stage

Tips

  • Do not stay on one Y-level for too long
  • Prioritize survival over greedy attacks
  • Rewarded ads may be used for continue/restart (if enabled)
